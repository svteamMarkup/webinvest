HOSTING.WEBINVEST provides worldwide multi-homed IP transit over an extremely fast network with virtually no latencies at unbeatable prices.
Advantages of HOSTING.WEBINVEST:
Our current network capacity is over 50Gbit per month and is constantly growing, enabling us to transfer our bulk-buying saving power onto our clients who enjoy unbeatable prices.
We use an array of premium Tier-1 providers such as Level3, AboveNet, TeliaSonera and more.

Flexible billing options for flat rate and burstable.
